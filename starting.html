Creating the lore of your nation

This is a role playing game, and to facilitate role playing you should answer some questions about your nation. Your nation’s flavor will grow organically throughout the campaign, but these answers will give you a starting point for roleplaying. Briefly describe your nation before the sundering.

  • Were you a large trading enclave, a warmongering empire, religious isolationists or something else?
  • Were you at war with any nations or worship any deities?
  • Who survived the sundering with you? Were the survivors all unknown workers in your nation, your personal guard, or noble bureaucrats? Did your family survive?
  • What do your people value (or what are their goals)? Do your people value intelligence over strength or do they follow “might is right”? Do you have a stratified society or are all men free?

For more information see the official rulebook.
